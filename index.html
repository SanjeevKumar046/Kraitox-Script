Guide

What?

A lineup of must-have programs, hand-picked just for you. You get to play app-roulette and decide which ones to bless your fresh Windows with, all thanks to our Winget wizardry. Because who says installing software can't be a bit whimsical? Let the app adventure begin! Bon appétit, Señor

Why?

I am aware that there are tens if not hundreds of similar apps/scripts that offer more or less the same functionalities. Why should anyone switch or use this instead of a well known and trusted one that does the same things and perhaps even offers more? A solid question. They shouldn't. Especially when we are talking about privacy in an script created by an internet stranger. However, there are passengers for every train and if you would like explore something new then you are welcome to try out mine. Simply put, Why not ?

How?

# Pre-requisites

  • > A computer running Windows.
  • > A caffeine supply adequate for at least three all-nighters.
  • > An irrational belief that this time, the installation will be smooth.
  • > Most importantly, Patience level of a Master Zen Monk.

# Steps

  • Step 1: Select the apps you desperately need.
  • Step 2: Click the big, shiny "Get your script" button to summon your custom script.
  • Step 3: Open PowerShell as the almighty administrator on your PC. It demands respect.
  • Step 4: Copy the path of your script. Paste it on the shell.
  • Step 5: Execute your script in PowerShell. Time to let it flex its muscles.
  • Step 6: Double-check the list of apps you chose to install. No surprises, just the apps you ordered.
  • Step 7: Voila! Sit back and relax while the script does the heavy lifting. It's like having minions, but for software.
